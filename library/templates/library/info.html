Welcome to Your Gamer Library!

Thank you for using Your Gamer Library to manage your gaming collection. Below are instructions on how to use the app:

1. Add a Platform:
To add a gaming platform, navigate to the platform section and enter the platform name. Click the "Add Platform" button to include it in your library.
2. Add a Game:
In the game section, provide the name of the game you want to add. Click the "Add Game" button to add it to your collection.
3. Add to Wish List:
If there's a game you wish to have in the future, use the wish list section. Enter the item details and click "Add to Wish List" to keep track.

Thank you for using Your Gamer Library. Enjoy managing your gaming collection!
